Aidocmaker.com's AI document creator streamlines the process of crafting professional documents.

Whether you're drafting a report, proposal, or summary, our AI-powered tool helps you generate content effortlessly.

## Essential Steps

1. **Go to the Document page**\
   Navigate to the [Generate Document page](https://www.aidocmaker.com/g0/file) on the Aidocmaker.com website.

2. **Select Your AI Model**\
   We recommend using "Auto Mode" which automatically selects the best AI model for your task.\
   Alternatively, you can manually choose from available models like ChatGPT 4.1, ChatGPT o4-mini, Gemini 2.5 Pro, Claude Sonnet 4 or Claude Opus 4.

  src="/getting-started/images/generate_document_models.png"

  className="rounded-2xl"
/>

3. **Enter Instructions**\
   Clearly specify what you want the document to include. For example:
   - "Create a 5-page sales sheet for the Canon imageCLASS LBP236dw printer"
   - "Create a summary of the attached financial report focusing on key performance indicators."

<img
  src="/getting-started/images/generate_document_instructions.png"
  alt="Document Instructions Interface"
  className="rounded-2xl"
/>

4. **Optimize Your Prompt**\
   Click the "Optimize" button to refine your instructions for better results.

5. **Generate the Document**\
   Click the "Generate" button and let the AI craft your document.

  src="/getting-started/images/generate_document_results.png"

## Quality Enhancements

To get even better results from your document generation, consider these additional steps:

1. **Provide Background Information**\
   Offer context such as the document's purpose, target audience, or any specific details to guide the AI.

<img
  src="/getting-started/images/generate_document_background.png"
  alt="Background Information Interface"
  className="rounded-2xl"
/>

2. **Upload Reference Files**\
   Attach relevant documents (e.g. PDFs, DOCX, XLSX) that the AI can use as references during generation.

<img
  src="/getting-started/images/multimodal_imports.png"
  alt="File Upload Interface"
  className="rounded-2xl"
/>

3. **Review and Export**\
   Once generated, review the content. You can make edits directly within the platform and export the final document in your preferred format.

## Tips & Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: The more detailed your instructions, the more tailored the output.
- **Provide Context**: Including background information helps the AI align the content with your objectives.
- **Use Reference Materials**: Uploading relevant files can enhance the accuracy and relevance of the generated content.
- **Iterate as Needed**: If the first draft isn't perfect, refine your instructions and regenerate.

By following these best practices, you can save time, reduce manual drafting, and consistently generate high-quality, tailored documents.
